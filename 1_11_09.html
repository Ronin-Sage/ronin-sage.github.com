::"Our Hunt Begins" -- January 11, 2009::

So here's the situation: 'academics are *not* looking good right now, and circumstances have me putting face-to-palm very frequently as of late. I won't(or rather, can't) take any classes this semester and am livin' off-campus for the time being, so the student-worker job I adored, financial aid, and any sense of stability/security I once had has all but decayed from the fallout of my failure.

Like a newborn forced unwillingly into the Light, I enter true adulthood.

Okay, so first-things-first--I need a reliable income, and that means getting a job. Mind you, I don't think I forsaw myself being a near- college-dropout, but I don't have the luxury of being taken aback--survival has to take priority, as I'd rather not even know the consequences of the alternative...
