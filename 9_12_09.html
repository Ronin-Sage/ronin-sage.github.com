++September 2009++

:: "I have returned" -- September 12, 2009 ::

It's been months since I've posted a journal entry here...

As far as the status quo, I've finally obtained a job, which in itself is cause for celebration considering my personal disposition and the job market as of late. But moreover, it's a job doing web design, something I have wanted to do professionally for quite some time now. There's some learning to be done, but I'm willing and able; the position pays notably well, too.

'next objective is to find an apartment or some such living area of my own.

I'm probably at a much better place than I've been in a long time in terms of mentality and disposition. Fortune, usually scornful and full of reproach, has smiled on me, at least momentarily.

"If a job's worth doing, it's worth dying for"
