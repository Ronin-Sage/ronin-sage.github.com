:: "Quakeify" Your Terminal ::

GUI. CLI. These are the two sibling deities that a user must pay tribute to. Any attempt to cross-worship is heresy to the extreme, so most users find themselves pledging allegiance to one or the other, with their respective prejudices defining them. But like anything, balance is the key here, so contrary to popular belief(yes, I'm talking about you #archlinux), it is possible for both to exist in harmony.

It's along these lines that establishes "quake-like/style" terminal emulators setups as my preference. For those unfamiliar with the concept, a quake-terminal is basically a terminal emulator that is "summoned" by rolling down from above the screen, and rolling back up beyond sight when the user hits a certain key. Thus is the behavior of "consoles" in pretty much every FPS game all the way back to the original Quake(as well as some non-FPS games, too, like UFO:AI).
